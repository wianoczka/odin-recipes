Ingredients for autumn tea:

  • 5 tea bags
  • 5 cups of boiling water
  • 5 cups of unsweetened apple juice
  • 2 cups cranberry juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon pumpkin spice

Steps

  1. Place the tea bags in a large heat-proof bowl; add boiling water.
  2. Cover and steep for 8 minutes.
  3. Discard tea bags.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients to tea.
  5. Stir until sugar dissolves.
  6. Serve warm or over ice.
